About The Legendary Falls

Adarna Falls is a seven-tiered waterfall shrouded in local mythology, named after the mythical Ibong Adarna from Filipino folklore. Each cascade features unique rock formations and natural pools said to have therapeutic properties from mineral-rich waters.

The main drop stands at 12 meters, creating a misty veil that forms rainbows on sunny days. The waters flow through volcanic rock formations dating back to Mount Pinatubo's eruptions, resulting in natural infinity pools at various elevations.

First opened to tourists in 2017, the falls are maintained by local indigenous communities who offer guided tours sharing ancestral stories about the site's spiritual significance.
