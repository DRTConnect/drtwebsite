About The Thermal Springs

Nestled at the foothills of Mount Pinatubo, Puning Hot Spring offers a unique blend of therapeutic geothermal baths and stunning volcanic landscapes. These natural sulfur springs maintain a constant 40-45¬∞C temperature, rich in minerals like sulfur and magnesium known for their healing properties.

The site features 12 open-air thermal pools with varying temperatures, surrounded by dramatic ash-colored rock formations from the 1991 eruption. A special highlight is the natural sand steam therapy area where guests are buried in volcanically heated sand.

Discovered in 2005 by local mountaineers, Puning has become a premier wellness destination combining adventure with rejuvenation.
