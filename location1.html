About This Natural Wonder

Verdivia Falls is Talbak's crown jewel, featuring a stunning three-tiered cascade surrounded by lush tropical vegetation. The falls get their name from the vibrant green hues of the water caused by mineral deposits and sunlight refraction through the canopy.

The main drop plunges 15 meters into a deep emerald pool perfect for swimming, while the upper tiers offer natural jacuzzi-like formations. The water maintains a refreshing 22¬∞C temperature year-round, fed by underground springs from Mount Talbak.

Discovered by local farmers in 2012, Verdivia has become famous for its "singing waters" - a unique acoustic phenomenon created by water flowing through limestone formations.
