About The Eco-Farm

Spread across 12 hectares of sustainable landscape, Avalon Eco-farm and Resort combines agricultural tourism with leisure facilities. Established in 2010, this family-owned property features organic farms, freshwater pools, and rustic accommodations powered entirely by renewable energy.

The resort's main attraction is its integrated farming system showcasing organic vegetable terraces, free-range poultry, and a tilapia hatchery. Guests can enjoy farm-to-table dining with ingredients harvested just hours before preparation.

Named after the mythical island of Avalon, the resort emphasizes environmental stewardship with zero-waste policies and nature-based recreational activities.
