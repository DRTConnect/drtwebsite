About This Natural Pool

Blue Lagoon is a breathtaking turquoise pool nestled in a limestone rock formation, renowned for its crystal-clear mineral waters that maintain a constant 24¬∞C temperature year-round. This geological wonder was formed by centuries of natural spring water filtration through karst formations.

The lagoon features varying depths from 1.5m shallow areas perfect for children to 4m deep zones ideal for diving. Surrounded by native ipil-ipil and narra trees, the site offers natural shade and a serene atmosphere enhanced by the gentle sounds of flowing spring water.

Discovered in 2012 by local farmers, Blue Lagoon gets its name from the striking azure hue created by sunlight refracting through calcium-rich waters. It has since become a must-visit destination for both local and international nature enthusiasts.
